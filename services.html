Reminders & Pickup

  • Expect a text/call once your pet is done.
  • Grace period of 1.5 hours from the time you were informed.
  • Please pick them up as soon as possible to avoid boarding charges.
  • Before leaving, double check ears, butt, nails, etc. for your approval.

Late pick-up fee: ₱100 / hour

We may refuse services if:

  • Pet has bad temper / is growling or biting.
  • Clearly sick (pale gums, lethargic) or has ticks & fleas.
  • Large infected wounds or incomplete vaccines.
